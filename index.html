About Me

Bri Gabiola has always been drawn to marketing, business ventures, and problem solving. Having opted into the business world as a teenager competing in DECA, Bri naturally stepped into a B.S. degree in Marketing from University of Idaho. With a passion for travel, new experiences, and the restaurant industry she packed her bags for a new city, and started learning the intricies of restaurant operation the best way she knew how; serving. As life moved forward and dreams altered course the passion for restaurants shifted to a side burner for more professional development to ensue.

If it couldn't presently be the restaurant industry it had to be something else that sparked joy, working as a Lead Listing Specialist for a vacation rental propery management company called Vacasa.

Who doesn't like working with vacations? Learning an immense amount of knowledge about travel industry, business growth, and the high demand for techology sparked a new dream.
